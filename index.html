About
What is TRAC?

In the wake of the pandemic, the landscape of our professional lives has undergone a profound transformation. Remote work has become the cornerstone of our operations, with our talented teams now spread across various states, regions, and countries. This shift has not only broadened our horizons but also fostered a culture of flexibility, collaboration, and innovation across Ringier AG tech teams.To harness the full potential of this new way of working, we introduced TRAC in 2022, our annual Tech, Retreat and Conference. TRAC is designed to unite our global teams, fostering connections and collaboration across the Ringier AG tech ecosystem.

Why is TRAC held every year?

This event serves multiple purposes:

  • Connection: Bringing together colleagues and tech teams from around the world to strengthen our community and build lasting relationships.

  • Motivation and Excitement: Inspiring and energising our teams for the new year, creating a shared sense of purpose and enthusiasm.

  • Exploration and Free Time: Providing opportunities to explore the host city, enjoy free time, and connect with other Ringier colleagues in a relaxed setting.

  • Strategic Alignment: Setting the stage for the year ahead by aligning activities, goals, and strategies to ensure cohesive progress and innovation across the Global Technology Unit (GTU)

  • Team Building: Engaging in activities that promote teamwork, trust, and camaraderie, enhancing our ability to work effectively together.

  • Learning: Hosting workshops and participating in workshops to learn new ways or perspectives on interesting technical topics

FAQ's

Let's answer some FAQ's

What documents will I need when traveling?
  • Approved South African visa (where applicable) 
  • Flight Ticket
  • Yellow Fever certificate (only applicable for Nigeria & Kenya)
  • Valid Passport
  • Travel insurance
  • Proof of accommodation
What should I know about check in at the Airport?
International guests
  • Make sure you check in 24 hours before you depart online via the airline you are flying with.
  • Be at the airport at least 3 hours prior to departure time. 
  • If you are travelling via Johannesburg or Cape Town as a connecting flight, please make sure you check-in your luggage at the interconnecting airport.
  • Gates close 30 minutes before departure time (depending on the airline - check your ticket for the details).
Local guests - flying from other cities in South Africa other than Gauteng
  • Make sure you check in 24 hours before you depart online via the airline you are flying with. 
  • Be at the airport at least 1 hour prior to departure time (make provision for possible traffic etc.);
  • On a Monday morning, the lines to check-in security usually are very long and that is why we would strongly advise those who are flying at 6 am, to be at the airport by no later than 04h50.
  • Gates close 30 minutes before departure time. (depending on the airline - check your ticket for the details).
Some travel essentials:
  • Don’t check valuables in checked-in luggage. Keep all valuables such as laptops, notebooks, jewellery etc. in hand luggage. 
  • Please lock your luggage and add a label on your suitcase. 
  • For international flights: Airline regulations require that you can bring your liquids and creams into no more than 100ml quantities. You'll need to place these in a clear 1L plastic zipped bag. Here's a guide on packing liquids. Note there are prohibited items in luggage - you'll see this list when you check in online.
  • Bring your laptop, charger, ​international plug adapter (or get one at the airport).
  • Sim cards are not essential as you have WiFi at the office, hotel, and many public places. If you want to, you can buy a sim card with data and call minutes for under $10.
What is the travel time?
It takes approximately 2 hours and 30 minutes from the airport to Sun City.
Airport Transfers:
  • Shuttle schedule will be released in the coming weeks once all flights have been booked.
  • We will travel in groups.
  • Please be sure to be at the collect and pick up points in time for departure to Sun City and departure on the last day to the Airport.
Can I extend my trip?
You can extend however all arrangements must be handled by yourself if you are not staying at Sun City. If you are bringing your family along, you will be responsible to make own arrangements for them. All additional expenses will be at your own cost.
We advise against bringing family along, however, if you do, let them join on the Thursday.  Non-attendees will not be able to join conference, team-building activities and year-end party.
